About the Log Application

This application is designed to simplify the process of analyzing and managing system logs. You can upload log files in .log or .txt format, and the system will help you visualize and extract useful information.

Future updates will include advanced tools for anomaly detection, trend analysis, and more. Stay tuned for additional features that will make your log management even more efficient.
